Variables:
1) Cannot start with numbers
2) Other than the $ & _ we can't able to use any special symbols. We can declare the $ & _ alone as variable. But for _ print statement throws an error
3) Doesn't contain spaces

Types of Variables:
1) Local Variables
2) Global Variables (also known as instance variable)
3) Static Variables 

Type Casting:	
1) Narrow typecasting (reducing the datatype)
2) Widening  typecasting (increase the datatype)
